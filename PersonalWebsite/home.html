My Goals

Growing up I have always had a love and aptitude for building. This could be anything from toys to eventually houses and fine woodworking in my shop. This ability to understand visually in my head is what led me to software development. I can picture the design in my head like I was building a table or a wall. I have found so many parallels to physically building something to building a program with code. My goal and desire is to use my skills and continue my learning and growth to advance in the software development profession and define myself as a leader. I will work until this goal is achieved.
